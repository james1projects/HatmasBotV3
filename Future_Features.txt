Future Feature Ideas
====================

1. Hats Stock Market
--------------------
Hats have a floating exchange rate. 1 Hat isn't always worth 1 Hat.
The market value starts at 1.0x each stream and ticks up or down every few
minutes based on weighted factors:

  - Chat activity pushes it up (more chatters = stronger economy)
  - Wins push it up, losses pull it down
  - Big gambling wins inflate the market, jackpots cause a spike
  - Low chat activity slowly deflates it
  - Random events (market crash, gold rush) fire occasionally

Viewer commands:
  !invest <amount>  - Convert Hats into "shares" at the current rate
  !sell             - Convert shares back to Hats at current rate (profit or loss)
  !market           - Show current rate and trend (up/down/stable)
  !portfolio        - Show holdings and current P/L
  !short <amount>   - Bet the market goes DOWN (advanced/degenerate option)

Visual overlay: A line chart in OBS showing last 30 min of market movement.
Green when trending up, red when trending down. Ticker at the bottom showing
recent trades.

Why it's good: Ties every system together. Predictions, gamble, song requests,
raids, gameplay all feed into one shared economy. Turns passive viewers into
active participants. Chat splits into bulls and bears.

Estimated effort: ~2 sessions. Market engine first, then overlay and commands.
